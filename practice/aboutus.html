About Us

AT KURUWA CARE NEPAL, we envision a supportive community where every individual can access exceptional caregiving services, ensuring both their well-being and dignity. Our mission is to provide a reliable and user-friendly platform that connects families with skilled and compassionate caregivers, enhancing the quality of life for both caregivers and those they care for. We collaborate closely with local healthcare institutions to guarantee that our caregivers are not only well-trained but also certified, adhering to the highest standards of care.

Our innovative app offers real-time updates and monitoring, giving families peace of mind with constant visibility into the care their loved ones receive. We respect and incorporate local customs and languages into our services, ensuring that patients feel understood and comfortable. In addition, our platform includes a built-in alert system for emergencies, ensuring timely assistance when needed most.

Guided by our core values of compassion, excellence, integrity, and innovation, we are committed to providing care with kindness, striving for the highest standards, maintaining transparency, and continuously improving through technology and community feedback. At the heart of everything we do is a dedication to enhancing the caregiving experience for everyone involved.
